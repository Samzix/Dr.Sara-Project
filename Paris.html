Eiffel Tower

The Eiffel Tower is a wrought-iron lattice tower located on the Champ de Mars in Paris. It is one of the most recognizable landmarks in the world and a symbol of France.

Louvre Museum

The Louvre Museum is the world's largest art museum and a historic monument in Paris. It is home to thousands of works of art, including the famous painting Mona Lisa.

Notre-Dame Cathedral

Notre-Dame Cathedral is a medieval Catholic cathedral located on the Île de la Cité in Paris. It is known for its Gothic architecture and is considered one of the finest examples in the world.
