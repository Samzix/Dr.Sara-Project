The Great Pyramids of Giza

The Great Pyramids of Giza are ancient monumental structures located on the outskirts of Cairo. They are the only surviving wonders of the ancient world and are a must-visit when in Cairo.

The Egyptian Museum

The Egyptian Museum in Cairo is home to a vast collection of ancient Egyptian artifacts, including the treasures of Tutankhamun. It offers a fascinating insight into the rich history of Egypt.

The Cairo Citadel

The Cairo Citadel is a medieval Islamic fortress that overlooks the city. It houses several mosques, including the impressive Mosque of Muhammad Ali, as well as museums and panoramic views of Cairo.

Khan El Khalili Bazaar

Khan El Khalili is a bustling traditional bazaar in the heart of Cairo. It offers a unique shopping experience with a wide range of products, including spices, jewelry, textiles, and traditional crafts.
