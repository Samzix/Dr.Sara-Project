Sagrada Familia

The Sagrada Familia is a famous basilica in Barcelona. It is renowned for its unique architectural style, designed by Antoni Gaudí, and has become one of the most recognizable landmarks in the city.

Park Güell

Park Güell is a public park located on Carmel Hill in Barcelona. It showcases Gaudí's artistic vision and features colorful mosaic work, unique buildings, and beautiful panoramic views of the city.

Casa Batlló

Casa Batlló is a modernist building in Barcelona, also designed by Antoni Gaudí. Its striking façade and imaginative design have made it a popular attraction, known for its colorful mosaic tiles and organic shapes.

Barcelona Cathedral

The Barcelona Cathedral, officially known as the Cathedral of the Holy Cross and Saint Eulalia, is a Gothic cathedral in the Gothic Quarter of Barcelona. It is known for its impressive architecture and beautiful stained glass windows.
